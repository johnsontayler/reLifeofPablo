    This is reLifeofPablo -- the greatest Kanye fan web app of all time.

    Kanye West's Saint Pablo tour was one of the most groundbreaking, interactive concert experiences. Being on the general admission floor amongst all the Kanye fans, while Kanye's stage hovered above us, was transportive.

    This app is for those fans, to reexperience a sliver of that magic again. And for those who didn't get a chance to witness the presence of greatest, you can pretend a little here.
